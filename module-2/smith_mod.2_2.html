Chapter 1-Unsure, A Tale of Joseph

I was born and raised in Landover, Maryland, Prince Georges County. I am a child of silence, since I was in the womb. At least that is the information given to me by my mother. I realized that silence has made me unsure about my direction in life.

As I grew older I have aquired the skill to use my silence to observe my surroundings. My silence have kept me content, safe and comfortable in everything. At what cost will I allow my silence to keep me from acheiving my goals.

I have always had the desire and passion to create worlds and tell stories. I want my stories to be told via Video Games. I want to allow others to experience worlds beyond our own and follow a story through the eyes of another being.

In order to do so I need to figure out what I truly desire. I need to understand the path I am to take. I need to be loud and end my silence.
